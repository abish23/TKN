Our Story

Thirukoneswarar Nadanalayam was founded to preserve and teach the classical Indian dance form Bharatanatyam in Europe. For over a decade, our school has been a home for students seeking a deep connection between body, rhythm, and spirituality.

Led by Dr. Chandravathane, our school combines traditional values with modern pedagogy. Every step and movement is an expression of inner devotion - dance as a prayer in motion.

Dr. Chandravathane

Dr. Chandravathane began her Bharatanatyam training as a child and completed her Arangetram under renowned masters in Tamil Nadu. With a passion for dance, discipline, and expression, she founded Thirukoneswarar Nadanalayam to share the beauty of this art form.

She leads workshops, performances, and training sessions across multiple cities and mentors her students individually along their artistic journey.
